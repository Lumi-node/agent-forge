## 🚀 Overview

AgentForge provides a lightweight, modular framework designed to help you rapidly prototype and understand the core mechanics of intelligent agents. We focus on building the fundamental reasoning loop—**Think $\rightarrow$ Act $\rightarrow$ Observe**—from the ground up, without the overhead of large, pre-built AI libraries.

Whether you are learning Reinforcement Learning, designing simple planning systems, or exploring autonomous behavior, AgentForge gives you the bare-bones structure to get started immediately.
